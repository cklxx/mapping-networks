CONCLUSIONS
1. Overfitting suppression from COMPRESSION — confirmed. Every mapping gap (0.8-1.25%)
   < baseline 1.47%; the 27M-param HyperNetwork (same architecture) overfits worst
   (2.55%). The control isolates it: fewer trainable params, not the architecture.
2. Accuracy parity NOT reached with this lazy mapping (fixed random projection ~ Li et
   al. 2018 intrinsic-dimension). Plateaus ~86% (d=4096), ~3pp under baseline. The
   paper's weight-modulation + stability/smoothness/alignment regularizers (not
   implemented here) are what they credit for closing it. High compression (414x, d=256)
   costs more accuracy (79.8%) — a clear accuracy/compression tradeoff.

-> Phase 2 (GSM8K math RL, §5.4 modulation of a PRETRAINED base) is the real target:
   fine-tuning a good base is more forgiving than from-scratch generation.
